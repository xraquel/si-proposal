Growth Marketing.

SEO

An effective SEO strategy requires both on-page and off-page activities. On-page SEO involves anything on your website which you control: content, structure of information, mobile experience, page load speed, and keyword optimization. Off-page SEO is the strategy you use to impact the relationship between related websites that reference your own. This means websites linking to you, referral traffic, social sharing, and brand mentions. Good SEO starts with an effective strategy. This means producing content that is optimized for your audience (on-page), then actively increasing the online visibility of your business through content marketing, PR, and engagement (off-page).

Pay Per Click Advertising (PPC)

Pay-per-click marketing is one of the most powerful internet marketing tools. Consumers use search engines to research, find and purchase products and services—it has become a necessary survival tool for most of the world’s population. Can you imagine a world without Google or it’s equivalents? As such, brands and businesses use paid ads as the quickest and easiest way of getting their company, product or service on the first page of search engines like Google.


Our PPC Advertising campaign strategies are designed to meet your needs and goals. Some clients prioritize top line sales while others prioritize profits/ROI. There isn’t a one size fits all strategy and each brand and business will have different strategies.


Generally, our #1 goal is to get the most out of your paid search campaigns. Whether this is done by writing great ads that connect with your ideal customers or optimizing the campaigns so they can run at the highest level, our team of PPC specialists ensure that your ads are performing well.


We also focus on remarketing which is a way of connecting with visitors to your website who may not have had the intention of making an immediate purchase or inquiry. We launch campaigns that focus these leads as they browse elsewhere on the internet. These campaigns generally have a higher conversion rate as these customers have previously expressed interest in your business, product or service— after all, there was a reason for them to have come to your website in the first place. This is the most powerful and highly cost-effective method of advertising that helps to remind leads to purchase your product or service, all while increasing your company’s brand awareness.
